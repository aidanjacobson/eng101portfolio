About Me

I was born in San Jose, California, on December 24th, 2001. At age 9, I moved to Scottsdale, Arizona, where I now live whenever I'm not at school. Currently, I am a Computer Science major at Arizona State University. I applied to ASU because we get in-state tuition rates. When I graduate, I plan to become either a web developer or work in cybersecurity. (If you're reading this, I built this website from scratch!)

I consider myself to be a good writer, however, I don't write that often. I think of myself as a person with a wide vocabulary, a flair for phrasing, and a lack of editing skills. It's not unusual for me to struggle with the "revision" part of the writing process, so I try to do my best to get it right the first time around. While I don't have a lot of reasons to write, when I do, I enjoy being able to express what I'm trying to say without necessarily having to say it to anyone. If there's one thing I'm good at, it's monologuing - and what better place to do that than the place where no one will stop you?

I'm taking First Year Composition because it is a required class for my general studies. That being said, I think I will get a lot out of this class. One thing I am looking forward to is the opportunities to exercise my writing muscles, and be able to be creative in my own way. Additionally, I believe this class will give me the tools to succeed in my other classes, when I inevitably have to write essays and paragraphs for less-than-exciting subjects, like science, or history.
